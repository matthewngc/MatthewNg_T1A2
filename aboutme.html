About Me

My Journey


My career began in the winter of 2017, during my second year of university, when I started my first job in the wealth management industry.
Over the next 3 years, I continued to work in this industry while finishing my degree, hoping that I could figure out what kind of career I want after graduating. On the completion of my degree, I was still unsure about what kind of future career path I wanted to pursue. I landed a graduate role in which I grinded through a year and a half of unsatisfying and unfulfilling work before finally deciding to take control of my future, and make a start in something I've always had an interest in.

In August of 2022, I resigned from my job and enrolled in Coder Academy, where I began the next chapter of my life in tech.

Work & Study History


I graduated from the University of New South Wales in 2020 with a Bachelor's degree in Actuarial Studies & Commerce, majoring in Finance and Accounting.

I got my first job in 2017 as an Account Manager at AMCO Strategic Financial Advice, where I assisted in the preparation and implementation of financial advice. After one year, I moved to Moore Australia as a Para-planner, where I helped in providing high level financial advice to medium-to-high net worth clients by performing financial modelling and market research. I stayed in this role for 2 years before starting as an associate with PwC Assurance, where I performed external audit procedures on large industry clients such as Sony Music Australia, Sony Interactive Australia and the Linde Group.

For more information about my work experience, please see my resume here.

Hobbies & Interests


I am an avid sports fan, having played basketball and soccer competitively growing up. I also enjoy creating and listening to music, being proficient in piano, guitar and saxophone. Outside of work, I like to travel, play video games, and spend quality time with friends and family.
